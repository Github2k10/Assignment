"Family Guy" is an American animated sitcom created by Seth MacFarlane for the Fox Broadcasting Company. The show premiered on January 31, 1999, following Super Bowl XXXIII and quickly established itself as a parody of more traditional sitcoms . The series was canceled in 2002 after three seasons but was later revived in 2005 due to its cult following .
The show revolves around the Griffin family, consisting of Peter, Lois, Chris, Meg, Stewie, and Brian. The lives of these characters have gone through various drastic changes in tone, style, and self-awareness over the years 4 . Some notable storylines include the controversial arc where Quagmire learned to be supportive of his transgender father who wanted to undergo gender reassignment surgery, which was publicly criticized by groups like GLAAD 4 .
